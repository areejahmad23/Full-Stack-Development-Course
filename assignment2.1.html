Dragon Fruit Smoothie Recipe

Dragon Fruit Smoothie Recipe from Sun Kissed Kitchen

I love dragon fruit. This smoothie would be very delicious.

Ingredients List

  • 1 cup dragon fruit
  • 1 cup mango
  • 2 bananas
  • 3/4 cup almond milk
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 1/2 scoop whey powder
  • 2-4 ice cubes

Directions on how to prepare

  • Step 1, add frozen fruit and milk and lime juice and protein powder to the blender
  • Step 2, Add more milk if necessary to thin to desired texture
